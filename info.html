FAQs

Q: What's the weather like?

A: Generally Newport Beach will be between 60 and 70 degrees in November. There is a chance there will be light winds.

Q: Is there parking?

A: There is a large free parking structure at the venue and a loading zone right in front of the reception area.

Q: Will there be alcohol?

A: There will not be alcohol at the venue. Please refrain from bringing your own alcohol!

Q: Will we be indoors or outdoors?

A: The ceremony will be outdoors on the lawn, the cocktail and coffee hour will be outdoors by the community room, and the reception will be indoors.

Q: Do I have a plus one?

A: The RSVP email you received should list everyone who is invited. Unfortunately, due to spatial limitations we cannot take plus ones.
